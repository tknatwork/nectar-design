Voice — do & don't

The accent strip on the left of each card is the engine's accent color (do) or a fixed warning hue (don't). The hex literal on the don't card is the only hex value on this page that isn't oklch().

Do

  • Plain, declarative sentences.
  • Show the receipts — real numbers, current numbers.
  • Pair the what with the why.
  • First-person when the work is yours. "I'm a tinkerer."
  • Sentence case for headings; Title Case for buttons (live convention).
  • Oxford commas. Curly quotes in prose.
  • Document the audit. audit/INCONSISTENCIES.md is part of the system.

Don't

  • "Stunning, powerful, next-generation."
  • "Leverages a sophisticated pipeline."
  • Three-word adjective stacks; exclamation points.
  • Quoting Steve Jobs or Dieter Rams.
  • "Are you sure?" Use a verb instead — "Delete?"
  • Inventing a "brutalist controls + glass" pairing. There is no brutalism on the live site.
  • Hardcoding hex outside the two Ink-Route constants.
