## Anatomy

A popover is a compound component: `PopoverTrigger` anchors it, and
`PopoverContent` portals a positioned surface with a pointing `Arrow`,
composing `PopoverTitle` / `PopoverDescription` / `PopoverClose`. The `side`
prop (`top` / `bottom` / `left` / `right`) picks the edge; the panel scales and
fades on open and close.

## Usage

- **Do** — use a popover for contextual, dismissible extras (account details, a share menu) anchored to the control that opened it.
- **Don't** — put critical, must-resolve actions in one; it closes on outside click and ESC, so anything mandatory belongs in a Dialog.

## Accessibility

- Base UI moves focus into the open panel and returns it to the trigger on close.
- `Escape` and an outside click both dismiss; the trigger exposes `aria-expanded` / `aria-controls` for the panel.
- `PopoverTitle` / `PopoverDescription` supply the panel's `aria-labelledby` / `aria-describedby` — render a title for a named region.
- Use the **Accessibility** tab on any story canvas for the live axe audit.
